As the qoute above shows, this page will talk about family... Specifically, my family

    My father

    Growing up in a Catholic household, my father is the youngest of eight. He was an athlete in school and stayed active after college.

    He is a kind but serious man, someone who values an honesty and character.

    Parenting style: Independence.

    He raised my brother and me to be able to stand on our own — so that when we grow up, we’re not

    fragile birds waiting for mom and dad to feed us. We survive. On our own.






    My mother

    My mother, the eldest of three, in a Catholic household. When she wasn't in school, she was helping around the house or on the farm. Hard work was just life.

    She is a disciplined woman. She expects proper manners, keep the house clean, and always be presentable.

    She reminds me to shave my beard. Every. Time.

    My brother

    My only younger brother. Five years apart.

    He plans to take Civil Engineering in college.

    Sometimes, he's just like me: into games, creative, stubborn, hot-headed.

    Other times, he is the total opposite: more artistic, better visual, artist, and does not have my passion for anime.

    But still, he's my brother.

    My only brother.
