  • My father

    • Growing up in a Catholic household, he is the youngest child with seven older siblings. He was an athlete during his school days and was still a bit after graduating college.

    His parenting method is raising my brother and I with independence.

    So that once we are older, he knows that my brother and I can survive on our own without relying too much on our parents.











  • My mother

    brother and I. Often, she wants me to shave my growing beard.
